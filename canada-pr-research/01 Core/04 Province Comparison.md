# Province Comparison

## Purpose
This note compares realistic province and region options at a high level before deeper research is added to the individual province notes.

## Comparison Criteria
- Cost of living
- Immigration friendliness
- Job market realism
- Suitability for weaker English
- Competition level
- Mental health / pressure considerations
- First-year survival likelihood
- Overall fit for my profile

- Use: `High`, `Medium`, `Low`, or `To Research`
- Add short evidence notes instead of vague impressions

## Early Working Questions
- Is Alberta the best balance between cost, job options, and pathway visibility?
- Are Manitoba and Saskatchewan more realistic than they first appear?
- Are Ontario and British Columbia too expensive and competitive for a cautious entry strategy?
- Do Atlantic provinces offer lower pressure, or do they create job and isolation risks?
