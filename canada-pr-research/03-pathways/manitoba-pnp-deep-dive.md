# Manitoba PNP Deep Dive

## Why Manitoba PNP is Considered More Accessible

### 1. No Job Offer Required (for most streams)
- Unlike BC PNP, Alberta AAIP, Atlantic AIP
- You can apply directly with your profile

### 2. Points-Based System
- Expression of Interest (EOI) scoring
- Factors: age, education, work experience, language, adaptability
- No specific cutoff announced publicly

### 3. Lower Competition
- Smaller population than Ontario/BC
- More willing to nominate candidates
- Government actively wants immigrants

### 4. "Connection to Manitoba" Helps
- Previous visit (you visited Canada in Jan-Feb 2026 - did you go to Manitoba?)
- Family in Manitoba
- Work experience in Manitoba
- Education in Manitoba
- Even: Job search in Manitoba, Intent to live there

## Your Profile vs Manitoba Requirements

### Skilled Worker in Manitoba Stream

- Post-secondary education in Manitoba (OR)

## Real Assessment: Is It "Easy"?

**The truth:**
- Not "easy" - still competitive
- But easier than Ontario/BC because:
  - Lower application volume
  - More predictable processing
  - Points-based (predictable)
  
**What you need:**
- Show genuine intention (visit, job search)
- Reasonable points score
- Clean background
