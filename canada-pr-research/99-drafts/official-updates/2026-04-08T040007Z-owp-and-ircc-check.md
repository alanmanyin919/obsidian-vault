## What Changed
- No new major IRCC change was found on 2026-04-08 beyond the already announced 2026 Express Entry category reset and the March 2026 Quebec temporary worker retention measure.
- The most relevant still-current PR planning change remains IRCC's 2026 category-based selection update announced on 2026-02-18. IRCC added categories for physicians with Canadian work experience, researchers with Canadian work experience, senior managers with Canadian work experience, transport occupations, and skilled military recruits, while continuing French-language, health and social services, STEM, trades, and education categories.
- The most relevant still-current work-permit-side update remains the 2026-03-13 Quebec measure. It is not a general OWP change. It is a Quebec-specific employer-specific work permit measure for some workers already on a Quebec PR path, with applications allowed until 2026-12-31.

## Relevance To My Situation
