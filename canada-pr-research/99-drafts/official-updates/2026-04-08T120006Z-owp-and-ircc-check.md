## What Changed
- No new major IRCC change affecting general OWP holders or standard Express Entry / CEC planning was identified on 2026-04-08 beyond recent February and March 2026 updates already posted by IRCC.
- The most recent OWP-related official change still visible is the March 23, 2026 update to family OWP eligibility. It adds a British Columbia significant investment project exception where a spouse may qualify for an OWP at any TEER level and without the usual 16-month remaining-validity requirement for the principal worker.
- The TR to PR pathway OWP page still shows that eligible 2021 pathway applicants can apply for this OWP until December 31, 2026.
- The most recent PR-planning change still visible is IRCC’s February 18, 2026 Express Entry category announcement. New 2026 categories include medical doctors with Canadian work experience, researchers with Canadian work experience, senior managers with Canadian work experience, transport occupations, and skilled military recruits. IRCC also said Canadian Experience Class draws continued through early 2026.
- A Quebec-specific transition measure announced March 13, 2026 allows certain workers with expiring employer-specific permits to apply for a new employer-specific permit tied to Quebec’s PR pathway process through December 31, 2026.
