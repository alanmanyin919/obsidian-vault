## What Did Not Change
- Alberta Opportunity Stream still requires a valid work permit and a full-time Alberta job offer, and it still does not accept maintained status or restoration status as qualifying work authorization.
- Rural Renewal Stream still requires a qualifying full-time job offer plus a valid endorsement letter from a designated rural community, and designated communities still operate with yearly endorsement allocations.
- Tourism and Hospitality Stream still remains employer-linked, including the requirement for current full-time Alberta work in an eligible occupation with an approved employer and at least 6 consecutive months with that employer.
- AAIP processing information still shows the same April 1, 2026 update date and the same 2026 priority posture toward employment-linked sectors and communities designated under the Rural Renewal Stream.
