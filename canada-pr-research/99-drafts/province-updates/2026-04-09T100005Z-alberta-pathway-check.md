- Tourism and Hospitality Stream still appears tightly employer-linked, and Alberta Opportunity Stream still depends on current authorized Alberta employment and a qualifying job offer.
- The Alberta government news pages did not show a newer immigration announcement after the April 1, 2026 oversight release that directly changed AAIP stream eligibility during this check.
