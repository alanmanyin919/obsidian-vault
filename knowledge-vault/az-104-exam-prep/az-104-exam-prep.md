## Study Approach
1. Read one topic note at a time.
2. Build or review the matching Azure resources in a sandbox subscription.
3. Update the lab checklist with what you have actually practiced.
4. Use the study plan to schedule revision and mock-question review.

## Focus Areas
- Azure identities and RBAC
- Governance with subscriptions, resource groups, tags, locks, and Policy
- Storage accounts, blobs, files, and access controls
- Virtual machines, disks, images, availability, and scaling
- VNets, subnets, NSGs, routing, DNS, load balancing, and connectivity
- Monitoring, alerts, backup, recovery, and operational troubleshooting
