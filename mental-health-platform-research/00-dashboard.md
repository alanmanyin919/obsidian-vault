# Mental Health Platform Research

## Purpose
This folder captures a research-driven direction for building a website that helps people under high pressure reduce stress and get safer support, without defaulting to a commerce-first model.

## Core Conclusion
The strongest direction is not:
- an open-ended AI therapist
- a generic mood tracker
- an unmoderated mental health forum

The stronger direction is:
- a structured stress-relief and support website
- focused on high-pressure environments
- built around evidence-based self-help tools
- with clear safety boundaries and escalation to human help when risk is high

## Recommended Product Direction
Build a web platform for:
- stress check-ins
- guided decompression exercises
- structured journaling
- CBT-style reframing tools
- workplace pressure recovery routines
- crisis and professional support routing
