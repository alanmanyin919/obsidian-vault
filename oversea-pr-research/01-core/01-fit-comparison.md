## Option Notes

### Hong Kong
- Best option for immediate income continuity and savings protection
- Easiest place to keep testing job strategy without taking relocation risk first
- Main weakness is that the current environment already appears harmful for mood, energy, and long-term motivation

### Canada (General)
- Main strategic advantage is that the OWP already lowers one major barrier compared with starting from zero
- Still risky because job landing path, support network, and first-year survival are unclear
- Best viewed as a controlled experiment rather than a one-way commitment

### UK
- Should be treated as a separate international option, not as part of the Canada comparison
- BNO is a real personal-profile advantage here, so the UK should not be treated as a generic unknown overseas option
- Even with BNO, it still needs separate review on job-market realism, cost of living, practical landing path, and whether the environment would actually be better for sustainability
- Current working view: more accessible than Australia on entry-path friction, but still not obviously safer than staying in Hong Kong or simpler than using the existing Canada OWP

### Australia
- Should not be assumed better or easier just because it is another overseas option
- Needs separate review on visa path, employment realism, cost, and mental-health sustainability
- For now it stays in scope only as a placeholder alternative, not a preferred path

## Working Decision Logic

### Most Defensible Current Position
- Hong Kong remains the safest operating base for short-term testing
- Canada remains the strongest upside option currently in scope because of the existing OWP
- UK remains a serious branch because BNO changes the entry-path discussion, but employability and survival math still need checking
- Australia should stay open only as a research branch until its practical barriers are checked

### What Looks Risky Right Now
- Direct relocation with no job offer, weak support, and only HKD 100,000 deployable budget
- Choosing the UK too early based only on BNO without checking job-market and survival math
- Treating Australia as a fallback without confirming visa and employability realities

### Best Current Use Of This Note
- Use Hong Kong as the baseline option
- Use Canada, UK, and Australia as separate branches that may later be ruled in or out
