# Settlement Preferences

## Purpose
This note is for clarifying settlement preferences that affect province choice, move style, and first-year survival planning.

## Current Status
- Status: Still exploring
- Rule:
  - Do not assume major-city preference or regional openness until it is reviewed deliberately

## Questions To Answer
- Am I open to smaller cities?
- Am I open to rural or designated-community pathways?
- Am I open to Atlantic provinces?
- Do I need a larger city for mental-health, convenience, or job reasons?
- Am I willing to use shared housing if that improves affordability?

## Why This Matters
- Settlement preference affects province shortlist
- Settlement preference affects financial planning
- Settlement preference affects mental-health risk and daily-life pressure
- Settlement preference affects which PR pathways are actually realistic

## Working Rule
- Do not choose a location based only on immigration talk
- Do not reject smaller or regional options before checking cost, job reality, and mental-health fit together
