# AAIP

## Purpose
Working note for Alberta Advantage Immigration Program research.

## Why This Path Matters
- Alberta is a realistic comparison province and has multiple streams worth tracking

## Research Status
- Last updated: 2026-04-04
- Current view:
  - Alberta remains relevant
  - Alberta should not be simplified into one stream or one shortcut

## To Research
- Current stream structure
- Which streams fit OWP-based entry scenarios
- Occupation and employer constraints
- Language and status requirements
- Whether Alberta remains practical after full cost and job reality review

## Working Interpretation
- Alberta pathways to compare:
  - [[alberta-opportunity-stream]]
  - [[alberta-express-entry-stream]]
  - [[rural-renewal-stream]]
- Current practical ranking for research:
  - Alberta Express Entry Stream: worth tracking if an Express Entry profile becomes viable
  - Rural Renewal Stream: may be stronger if a designated community and employer fit can be found
  - Alberta Opportunity Stream: useful only if permit type, job offer, occupation, and Alberta work conditions all line up
- Alberta should be compared directly with Atlantic and other regional routes, not assumed better by default

## Evidence Notes
- Official Alberta program pages reviewed on 2026-04-04 confirm that worker-stream access is more conditional than a generic OWP narrative suggests
- The main unresolved question is whether the current OWP category fits Alberta's accepted work permit conditions for AOS
