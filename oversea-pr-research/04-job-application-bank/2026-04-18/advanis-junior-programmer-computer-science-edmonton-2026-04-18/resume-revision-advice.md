### 6. What Not To Add
- Do not claim strong Unix, TDD, or database-design depth unless you can support it honestly
- Do not rewrite the resume to sound like a senior enterprise architect when this role is explicitly junior and training-oriented
- Do not overemphasize Azure or enterprise platform terms if they distract from `JavaScript`, `Python`, `SQL`, and general programming fit
