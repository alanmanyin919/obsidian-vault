## Core Position Summary
- Software developer role focused on application support and application development
- Work includes new features, bug fixes, troubleshooting, business-user support, and process improvement
- Environment appears to value SDLC discipline, Agile ways of working, and evolving DevSecOps or DecSecOps practices

## Main Responsibilities
- Review software requirements and design documentation for new and existing applications
- Write clean, maintainable code and associated unit or automated tests for new features and bug fixes
- Contribute to SDLC, Agile, and DecSecOps process development
- Identify opportunities to improve business process efficiency
- Troubleshoot issues and recommend modifications to meet changing user requirements
- Provide guidance and support to business users on effective application usage and IT best practices

## Stated Requirements
- BSc in Computer Science, technical diploma specializing in programming, or equivalent
- `3+ years` of software development experience
- Experience in designing `REST`-based APIs
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
- Self-motivated and able to prioritize, meet deadlines, and manage changing priorities
- Proven ability to work independently and in a team environment
- Excellent oral and written communication skills
- Results-oriented and comfortable in a fast-paced environment

## Valued Assets
- Familiarity with `SDLC`, Agile, and `DecSecOps`
- Microservices and cloud service platforms, especially `AWS`
- Guidewire development experience, including `GOSU` and `Jutro`

- Previous experience in insurance or financial institutions
- `.NET` technologies
