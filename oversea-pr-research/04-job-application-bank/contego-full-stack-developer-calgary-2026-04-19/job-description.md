## Job Metadata
- Company: Contego
- Role: Full Stack Developer
- Employment type: Full-time, Permanent
- Pay: `$80,000-$100,000` per year
- Location: Calgary, Alberta, Canada
- Work model: Hybrid after 3 months, with minimum 1 day per week in office
- Work location wording: `Hybrid remote in Calgary, AB T2P 2X6`

## Core Position Summary
- Full-stack developer role with strong end-to-end ownership expectations
- The role covers the full software lifecycle from client conversations through deployment and post-launch support
- The environment includes web and Windows applications, client-facing communication, and implementation support

## Company Context
- Contego describes itself as a Calgary-based team building custom software solutions and business applications
- The company emphasizes problem-solving, systems improvement, and client-focused delivery
- The development team appears to be small enough that individual ownership is a major expectation

## Main Responsibilities
- Lead full-cycle development projects from needs analysis through post-launch support
- Conduct client interviews and translate business needs into technical solutions
- Create prototypes, design system architecture, and develop robust applications
- Deploy projects into production and support clients after implementation
- Collaborate with other developers as needed
- Provide technical support related to products and client implementations
- Create training environments and documentation for client onboarding

## Stated Requirements
- Minimum `5 years` of full-cycle application development experience
- Proficiency in `C#`, `.NET`, `.NET Core`
- Proficiency in `HTML` and `JavaScript`
- Experience with `MS SQL` or other relational databases
- Experience with API integrations such as `JSON` and `XML`
- Experience with `Visual Studio`, `SSMS`, and `Postman`
- Experience with `SharePoint` and `Power Apps`
- Solid understanding of `SAML` and secure authentication protocols
- Strong communication skills with ability to interface with C-suite stakeholders
- Ability to work independently and manage time effectively

## Preferred Or Asset Signals
- Experience with accounting systems
- Experience with `Acumatica`
- Experience with `Microsoft Dynamics SL`
- Experience with `FoxPro`
- Comfort working with or maintaining legacy codebases
- Post-secondary education in a technical field
- `English` preferred

## Application Constraints
- Hybrid work starts only after 3 months
- Minimum 1 day per week in office after the transition
- C-level client communication is part of the role
- Strong self-management is expected

## Employer Signals
- This is not an entry-level developer role despite the broad title
- The employer appears to want a client-facing developer who can own delivery independently
- Business systems, authentication, internal tools, and legacy environments seem more central than modern product-engineering specialization

## Immediate Screening Signals
- Positive signal: strong overlap in APIs, relational databases, enterprise application work, and documentation
- Positive signal: secure authentication and Azure/Microsoft-oriented background may transfer partially into this environment
- Risk signal: `C#`, `.NET`, and `SAML` look like meaningful named expectations, not optional extras
- Risk signal: communication with C-suite stakeholders is explicitly required
- Risk signal: hybrid Calgary practicality is still a real barrier from Hong Kong
