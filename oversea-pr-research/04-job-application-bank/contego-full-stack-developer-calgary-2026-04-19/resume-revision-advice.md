## Main Positioning Angle
- Position yourself as an enterprise application developer with strong overlap in APIs, relational databases, implementation support, documentation, and business-system delivery
- Emphasize transferable fit into custom business applications rather than pretending direct mastery of every named Microsoft tool
- Keep the tone professional and ownership-capable, but avoid overstating C-suite communication confidence

### 6. What Not To Add
- Do not add `.NET`, `C#`, `SharePoint`, `Power Apps`, or `SAML` unless you can support them honestly
- Do not overstate C-suite client communication if the evidence is limited
- Do not make the resume sound like a pure Microsoft-stack consultant if that is not true
