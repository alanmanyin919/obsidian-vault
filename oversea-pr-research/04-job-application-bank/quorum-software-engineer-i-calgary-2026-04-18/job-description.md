## Core Position Summary
- Junior software engineering role in Quorum's Upstream Planning portfolio
- Focus on implementation support, code quality, learning best practices, and collaboration with senior engineers
- Environment includes Agile delivery, CI/CD, DevOps practices, and modern application development

## Company Context
- Quorum Software positions itself as the largest digital technology provider focused solely on business workflows for the energy sector
- The company serves customers across the energy value chain, from emerging companies to supermajors
- The role sits in an oil and gas transformation context rather than a generic software-only context

## Overview Wording From The Posting
- Quorum is hiring a Junior Software Engineer to assist with solutions across the Upstream Planning portfolio
- The role is intended to support technical tasks, learn best practices, and collaborate with senior team members
- The stated objective is to improve deliverable quality and performance

## Main Responsibilities
- Develop and maintain Upstream Planning solutions in an Agile environment under guidance
- Follow architectural standards, coding practices, and reusable component patterns
- Learn and apply modern technologies and frameworks
- Support implementation of technical solutions under coding and quality guidelines
- Assist with CI/CD pipelines and DevOps practices
- Collaborate with stakeholders, identify risks, and help unblock delivery
- Maintain documentation and comply with guidelines
- Perform other assigned duties as needed

## Stated Requirements
- 1+ years of software application development experience
- Working knowledge of `.NET`
- Working knowledge of `RESTful APIs`
- Understanding of object-oriented programming concepts
- Understanding of relational databases
- Understanding of DevOps practices and Agile methodologies
- Basic understanding of cloud concepts and platforms such as `Azure` or `AWS`
- Basic understanding of APIs and microservices architecture
- Basic understanding of security protocols and encryption standards
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
- Strong communication skills

## Preferred Skills
- Familiarity with AI coding tools

- Basic understanding of Model Context Protocol (MCP) or similar extensibility patterns
