## Main Positioning Angle
- Position yourself as a software engineer with `5+ years` building and supporting enterprise applications
- Emphasize transferable overlap: `REST APIs`, databases, Agile delivery, CI/CD exposure, Azure-connected systems, troubleshooting, and documentation
- Surface Canada work authorization near the top because this employer explicitly will not sponsor

### 6. What Not To Add
- Do not add `.NET`, `C#`, oil-and-gas domain knowledge, microservices architecture ownership, or security/encryption claims unless you can support them honestly
- Do not rewrite the resume to sound senior if the role is explicitly `Software Engineer I`
- Do not hide the Hong Kong base if you are not yet in Calgary; instead, present relocation readiness clearly
