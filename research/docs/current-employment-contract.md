## Document

**Private & Confidential**  
**Employment Contract**

The Employment Contract is made between **Eastech Systems Limited** (the "Company") and **Man Ho Yin, Alan**, holder of HKID Card No. `Y419963(2)` (the "Employee"), on **6 May 2025**, whereby it is mutually agreed and bound by the following:

## 1. Definition

"Company" means:

- a company formed and registered under the Company Ordinance
- an existing company

As defined by the Companies Ordinance (Cap. 32) of the Laws of Hong Kong SAR ("Companies Ordinance").

## 2. Appointment, Commencement Date and Salary

The Company will employ the Employee as **System Developer in PS - Data & AI Division** commencing on **6 May 2025**. The monthly base salary will be **HK$28,000.00**, payable in arrears within the first seven (7) days of the following calendar month.

The Employee shall perform duties in connection with the business of the Company. In addition to the duties in the job details, the Employee is expected to undertake additional duties from time to time reasonably assigned and communicated by the Company to meet business needs. Notwithstanding one's role and levels of responsibility, duties may be significantly varied throughout employment.

## 3. Bonus

Discretionary bonus would be provided based on individual and the Company's performance. If one's employment is ceased on or before the bonus payment date, the Employee will not be entitled to any bonus.

## 4. Conflict of Interest

During the employment period under this contract, one will not undergo other employment(s) except employment(s) specifically authorized by the Company. The Employee shall not directly or indirectly serve, engage in, be concerned with, or provide services to any other person, company, business entity, or other organization whatsoever, whether as an advisor, agent, consultant, independent contractor, or otherwise, that competes, conflicts, or interferes with the performance of one's duties or the business of the Company, unless there is prior written consent from the Company. Such consent may be withheld or revoked at the sole discretion of the Company at any time without any reasons.

## 5. Working Location

This Employment Contract will take place in the Hong Kong SAR. The Company reserves the right to require the Employee to work at offices or places outside HKSAR on a temporary basis as the Company may from time to time appoint duties to the Employee, in which one may need to travel from the original place of employment.

## 6. Working Hours

Monday to Friday: `0930 - 1830` hours, inclusive of one-hour meal break.

A Statutory or General Holiday ("Public Holiday") falling on a Saturday will be regarded as a Statutory or Public Holiday and no substituted holiday for the Saturday off will be granted.

The Employee may be required to work beyond the office hours stated above to cater to business needs. There will be no overtime payment.

## 7. Probation

The Employee shall undergo a probation period of **three (3) months** or such extended period as the Company may consider necessary. On satisfactory completion of this period, the appointment will be confirmed in writing.

## 8. Holidays

The Employee is entitled to Public Holidays as published in the Government Gazette.

## 9. Annual Leave

Upon completion of the probation period, the Employee is eligible for a full paid annual leave of **twelve (12) days for every 12-month period** following the Commencement Date. The number of annual leave will be increased progressively in accordance with years of service, details of which can be found in the Company Policy.

## 10. Mandatory Provident Fund Scheme ("MPF Scheme")

The Company will enroll the Employee in the Company's MPF Scheme. The Company and the Employee will make mandatory contributions to the MPF Scheme in accordance with Hong Kong law.

## 11. Attendance

Attendance is recorded every day by each department. Employees who are unable to report for work are required to seek approval from their department head(s) with valid reasons **1/2 (0.5) hour** before the normal shift commences. Absence for **two (2) or more consecutive working days** without prior approval would be regarded as resignation and adequate payment in lieu of notice must be given to the Company in accordance with paragraph 16 hereof.

## 12. Medical and Life Insurance

Upon completion of the probation period, the Employee is eligible for Group Medical and Life Insurance. Detailed information can be found in the Company's intranet.

## 13. Company Policies

The Employee shall at all times act in accordance with the **Employee Handbook** as well as all Company circulars, notices, policies and procedures issued by the Company and uploaded on the Company's intranet from time to time. The terms and conditions contained in the Employee Handbook and other Company circulars, notices, policies and procedures will be updated by the Company as circumstances may require.

## 14. Confidentiality

The Employee shall not:

- at any time during employment, except so far as necessary and proper in the course of employment; or
- at any time after employment,

disclose any trade secret or non-public information including, without limitation, the Company's or any of its Associated Companies' technology, know-how, business practice, dealings, affairs, customer information, or confidential information belonging to third parties with which the Company has executed a non-disclosure agreement and of which the Employee has or should have knowledge, and any information the disclosure of which would violate any government regulatory agency requirements in the Hong Kong SAR ("Confidential Information").

The Employee shall comply with the terms contained in the Confidentiality Statements. Any disclosure of employment terms and conditions to other employees or outside parties constitutes a breach of this contract and may result in disciplinary action.

Upon request of the Company at any time, the Employee shall immediately:

- return or procure the return to the Company, as directed, of all hard copy Confidential Information in the Employee's possession;
- destroy or procure the destruction of all copies of any note, analysis, calculation, study, or other document containing or derived from any Confidential Information; and
- erase or procure the erasure from any computer or electronic device under the Employee's control of any document or file containing Confidential Information in any manner that makes the erased data permanently irrecoverable.

Immediately upon completion of these matters, the Employee shall, upon request of the Company, confirm in writing that the requirements of this paragraph have been complied with.

Any breach or failure to observe these conditions will make the Employee liable to immediate dismissal without compensation, and the Company reserves the right to take any other action it considers appropriate.

## 15. Non-Solicitation

For a period of **twelve (12) months** after termination of the contract, for any cause whatsoever, the Employee shall not canvass, solicit, or endeavour to take away from the Company the business of any customers of the Company who have been customers of the Company during the period of twelve (12) months immediately preceding the termination of employment or with whom the Employee shall have come into contact in the performance of duties during that period.

The Company specifically includes customer information as Confidential Information as defined above. The Employee is also prohibited from hiring or attempting to hire any of the Company's employees or staff.

## 16. Termination of Employment

During the probation period, termination of employment within the first **one (1) month** may be made by either the Employee or the Company immediately in writing. After the first one (1) month of employment, termination may be made by either party with **seven (7) days'** prior written notice or an equivalent amount of wages for the notice period.

After the probation period, either party may terminate employment by giving **one (1) month's** prior notice in writing or an equivalent amount of wages for the notice period. For clarity, termination of employment shall not affect those clauses intended to survive termination of employment contract, including without limitation confidentiality, non-solicitation, non-competition, and any confidentiality statement executed between the parties, all of which are incorporated by reference.

All leave entitlements may not be used to offset notice for termination of employment without the Company's approval. The notice period should not include any kind of leave. If leave is taken, payment by the Employee to the Company in lieu of notice or extension of the notice period may be required accordingly at the sole discretion of the Company.

## 17. Miscellaneous

In this contract, unless the context otherwise requires, the singular includes the plural and conversely; words importing any gender shall include the other gender and the neuter.

This contract shall be governed by and construed in all respects in accordance with the laws of the Hong Kong SAR, and the parties hereby submit to the exclusive jurisdiction of the court of the Hong Kong SAR.

The Company welcomes your membership to a bright future and wishes you every success in the coming years with us.
